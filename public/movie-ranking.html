The Best Movies Of All Time, According to Ashish.

These are my top three favorite movies of all time.

Inglorious Basterds

A movie where a group of american soliders infiltrate nazis and brutally take down who ever they can.

John Wick

A movie where an Elite Assassin takes revenge on a crime boss after the son of the crime boss kills his Dog.

Iron Man

A movie about a Genius, Billionare, Playboy, and Philanthropist who invents a powerful yet compact energy source and uses it to power a high-tech suit. The Iron Man suit.
